# Members (team)

When you go from one person to a team, the **Members** screen is where you invite
people and manage who's who. *Team tier (F1-Starter): up to 10 members.*

## Roles

- **admin** — can invite/remove members and author access policy. The person who
  created the team is the **owner** (an admin who can't be removed).
- **member** — uses the mesh; sees the services your policy grants them.

## Invite a teammate (admin)

1. On **Members**, click **Create invite link**.
2. **Copy** the link and send it to your teammate.

The link looks like `ankayma://join-team?token=…`. It's good until you hit your member
cap.

## Join a team (the invitee)

1. **Sign in** to Ankayma with your own GitHub account first.
2. On **Members → Join a team**, paste the invite link (or just the token) and click
   **Join**.

From then on, signing in puts you **into that team** — you see the team's services and
act under your team role. (At this tier you belong to one team at a time.)

## Remove a member (admin)

Click **Remove** next to a member. This is the offboard switch: the moment you remove
them, their **next request loses team access** — no waiting for a session to expire.
Their account drops back to its own personal space.

> Tidy-up: removing a member revokes their *access*. If they had enrolled devices in
> the team, retire those separately on the [Devices](devices.md) screen.

## Cap reached?

The counter at the top (e.g. `7/10`) shows usage. At the cap, invites are refused until
you remove a member or move to a higher tier.
